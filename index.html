Writing Imitation Practice

Welcome to Writing Imitation Practice!

How it works:

  1. Paste your article and click "Start Practice".
  2. Rewrite the displayed sentence from memory.
  3. Press "Enter" or click "Check" to compare your input.
  4. Progress is shown via the progress bar.

Shortcuts:

  • Enter: Check your input.
  • Alt: Temporarily reveal the original sentence.
