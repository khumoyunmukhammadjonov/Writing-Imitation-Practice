Writing Imitation Practice

Enhance your English writing skills through imitation, inspired by the shadowing technique used in language learning.

Download Chrome Extension

What is Writing Imitation Practice?

Writing Imitation Practice adapts the shadowing technique—where learners repeat audio to improve pronunciation—into a writing context. By imitating well-crafted sentences and structures, you internalize grammar, vocabulary, and cohesive essay patterns. This method is especially helpful for preparing for IELTS Writing Task 2 and other academic or professional writing tasks.

How It Works

After installing the extension, you can paste any sample essay or text into the practice area. The extension breaks the text into manageable chunks, allowing you to retype each sentence from memory. By comparing your output with the original, you identify mistakes and learn correct structures. The built-in timer and statistics help track your progress and keep you motivated.

Why It Helps

Imitating strong writing exposes you to a variety of sentence patterns, linking words, and academic vocabulary. Over time, this practice improves your fluency, accuracy, and confidence. Whether you're aiming for a higher band score in IELTS or simply want to enhance your English writing, this technique provides a structured and effective path.

How to Install

1. Extract the Zip

After downloading, locate Writing Imitation Practice(2).zip in your downloads folder. Extract the zip file to a convenient location on your computer.

2. Open Extensions Page

Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions. In the top-right corner, enable Developer mode.

3. Load Unpacked

Click Load unpacked, then browse to the folder you extracted earlier. Select the folder to install the extension. You’re ready to start practicing!
